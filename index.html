What are administrative changes?
Administrative changes are major changes which take place on an annual/rare basis. Some examples are:
  • Changes in the administration of the club (election of the president, change of teacher in charge, etc)
  • Change in contact details (email id, phone numbers)
NOTE: Administrative changes require a unique PIN to access. Kindly contact the developers for more information.
